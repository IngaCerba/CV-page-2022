My adventure with programming ...

... started basically inconspicuously. Learning programming did not result from a great, ardent passion or calling which many of my friends in the field are endowed with. It was born out of the need to understand not the code itself, but my man. Tyrion - coming home - loves to tell with commitment worthy of a novelist about the struggles and toils of battles at work. Unfortunately, at the beginning of our relationship, his colorful and multi-layered stories found their way to completely insusceptible ground, mainly because he was telling them in my direction, and I didn't understand them at all. Knowing that such a state of affairs cannot last forever, we made the only right and logical decision and together we started to discover the mysterious and unknown world of programming. The beginnings were shaky, but as I am an inquisitive creature by nature, new questions arose in me as I learned about coding, and Tyrion answered them with patience of a Tibetan monk. From conversations and individual articles on the Internet, we moved over time to online courses, and after them - to post-secondary school, which, to the surprise of my family and friends, I graduated with a satisfactory result. Feeling unsatisfied, I joined Codecademy for a longer time, where in my free time I complete the next courses. 3 years have passed since the beginning of this journey, during which coding turned from a harmless attempt to communicate into my dream career path.
